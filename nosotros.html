THE RED ARMY

The Red Army of Workers and Peasants RKKA, was the official denomination of the army and the air force of the Soviet Federative Socialist Republic of Russia and, after 1922, of the Soviet Union.

THE WEHRMACHT

Wehrmacht is the German word for Defense Force, from "wehren" (to defend) and "Macht" (strength). Therefore, in German, the term applies to any country and not only to the German armed forces, although the term has been widely used in publications in other languages to refer to the German armed forces of the period 1935-1945.

OPERATION BLUE

Operation Blue was executed by the German Wehrmacht in 1942. The objective of this operation was to take away the oil wells of the Caucasus from the Soviet Union.
